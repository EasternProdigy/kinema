Keyboard shortcuts

Most work while a video is playing. Press ? anytime to see this list.

  • Scrub previews: hover the seek bar to preview frames; chapter markers show as ticks you can jump between.
  • Audio & subtitles: pick embedded tracks from the audio and CC buttons in the control bar.
  • Sleep timer: the moon-clock button can stop playback after this episode or a set time.
  • Firefox PiP: hover the playing video and click Firefox's own picture-in-picture toggle, or use the ⧉ PiP button / press P.
  • Resume: your position is saved automatically and synced across tabs.
  • Non-MP4/WebM files are remuxed on the fly when ffmpeg is available.
