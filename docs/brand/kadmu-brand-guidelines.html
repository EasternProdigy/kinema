04 — Voice & Tone

how kadmu sounds

Calm, plain-spoken, on the viewer’s side. It’s your stuff on your machine — never hypey, never a streaming-service pitch.

01

Clear over clever

Say what the button does. The useful thing first.

02

Calm, not flashy

No countdowns, no “premium experience.” Just the video.

03

Human and direct

Talk like the friend who set up your media server.

We say

“Point Kadmu at a folder. Press play.”

“Your files stay on your machine.”

“Pop it out with Picture-in-Picture.”

We don’t

“Leverage our next-gen streaming platform.”

“Sign in to unlock your entertainment journey.”

“Buffering your premium experience…”
